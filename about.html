About me

Certified IELTS trainer, ESL and FLE (Français Langue Étrangère) teacher and translator based in Tehran, Iran

Questions? Ask me now! Questions? Contactez-moi!

My name is Nadia Bayat and I am 32 years old. I started teaching English in 2011 in various English institutes in Tehran. While doing my first degree in French Language Interpretation and Translation at Alzahra University, I decided to change my career path and become a French teacher, too. I obtained a certificate in teaching French as a foreign language in 2016.

After receiving my Master's degree in French Language Interpretation and Translation from University of Tehran in 2019, I decided to work as a freelancer and deliver online private lessons to students from around the world.

I have been teaching both English and French to children, young and adult learners ever since. I also help students with IELTS and TEF (Test d'évaluation du français) by practicing test skills through preparation courses to check students' progress in order to improve their actual language level to get the score they need.

As a highly motivated, enthusiastic and creative teacher with a proven record of helping students progress, I provide positive learning experiences as well as treating peers equally in the same situation, with 10+ years of experience teaching different age groups and levels of students.

Students can take advantage of this modern form of education and study online at their own pace. This is the reason why I became a virtual tutor to empower my students to become self-reliant learners. I am here to provide fun, engaging and relevant opprotunities that allow you to achieve accademic success. Let's make learning creative, flexible and challenging!
