English for Young Learners

These English courses are designed for 5-12 years old. Children learn how to speak, read and write in English through games, songs, stories, online worksheets and activities. Super Safari, Family & Friends, Oxford Discover and Oxford Phonics World are among English learning books suited for these courses. Students who seek to succeed in YLE (Young Learners) tests at Starter(Pre A1), Movers(A1) and Flyers(A2) can attend specific courses designed for test preparation.
